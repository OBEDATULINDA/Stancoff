STANCOFF UPDATE 6.4 - CASUAL PAYMENT RECEIPT

This update changes only the printable casual payment receipt.

The receipt now includes:
- Payment date
- Attendance period
- Full days worked
- Half days worked
- Total equivalent days paid
- Payment method
- Payee acknowledgement statement
- Payee signature/thumbprint line
- Payer name and signature line
- Signature dates

No database table is deleted or replaced. Existing attendance, payments,
coffee operations, sales, inventory, users and passwords remain unchanged.
