STANCOFF UPDATE 6.8.1 — DRY WEIGHT STORAGE FIX

Finish Drying now records two separate quantities:
1. Wet/semi-dry weight being completed and removed from the drying balance.
2. Actual dry weight entering the selected storage location.

The difference is recorded as drying loss. Partial completion remains supported.
A safe nullable source_weight column is added automatically to coffee_movement.
No existing PostgreSQL tables or records are deleted.
