STANCOFF UPDATE 6.8 — UNIFIED COFFEE MOVEMENT ENGINE

WHAT CHANGED
1. The separate moisture-test approval step is not required.
2. Drying records now have a Finish drying action.
3. Finish drying lets the user select:
   - the current coffee balance,
   - weight to move (partial or full),
   - destination station,
   - destination temporary store or final warehouse.
4. Same-station storage creates a normal coffee movement.
5. Cross-station storage automatically creates both:
   - a coffee movement, and
   - a station transfer with a printable transfer note.
6. Inventory reduces at the source and increases at the destination.
7. Partial completion leaves the remaining coffee at its current location.
8. Existing PostgreSQL records and tables are preserved.

DEPLOYMENT
Extract the ZIP, upload the contents of Stancoff-main to the existing GitHub repository,
commit the changes, and allow Render to redeploy. Do not delete the PostgreSQL database
and do not change DATABASE_URL.
